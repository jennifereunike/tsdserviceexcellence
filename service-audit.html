SERVICE AUDIT / MYSTERY SHOPPING

Visit to a physical store by trained auditors to evaluate the overall performance of the staffs without their awareness.

HOW IT IS DONE:

  1. Set the specific areas and items to be checked.
  2. Design the questionnaire.
  3. Train auditors/mystery shoppers to conduct service audits.
  4. Get auditors to conduct service audits to the stores and fill in the questionnaire with detailed comments.
  5. Validate and check results.
  6. Prepare qualitative report 

WHAT YOU WILL GET:

  1. A quantitative report that summarizes the visual merchandising audit results.
  2. Individual audit results which include the questionnaire and photos taken by auditors in store.
