WHY SERVICE AUDIT?

In today's social-media driven era, customer experience defines the make-or-break of a brand. Happy customers will be your unpaid, passionate ambassadors; while unhappy ones will be ill-mouthers that drive customers away from your business. 

While you are confident that your staffs are well-trained, are you sure they will still reflect your high standards when their guards are down? Can you ensure every customer will get the 5-stars experience that you taught your staffs to give?

Service audits, or widely known as mystery shopping, will give you an inside look into the customer experience and provide you with a way to improve customer satisfaction. It gives insights on how to increase sales conversion, gain and retain more customers, increase sales, and increase customer loyalty. 

SERVICES

Visual Merchandising Audit

Unannounced, thorough physical check (ex: cleanliness, safety & security, defects, display compliance) of the entire store with photographic evidences.

Mystery Service Audit

Visit to a physical store by trained auditors to evaluate the overall performance of the staffs without their awareness.

Social Media Reviews

Gathering and analysis of social media reviews from real customers who visited the store.

Benchmarking

Visit to a competitor store by trained auditors to evaluate them based on similar indicators as your own service audits, then compare the results.

Training & Coaching

Training and coaching to staffs based on the insights from service audit and social media reviews.

Consulting

Review and restrategising of goals and KPIs based on the insights from audits, benchmarking and social media reviews.
